It's More Fun in the Philippines!

The Philippines, along with its thousands of islands, is known for its brilliant and outstanding
festivals: loud, colorful, and bursting with energy and life. In Western Visayas, some celebrations stand
out as the most awaited each year, bringing streets alive with music, dance, and tradition. From the vibrant
Ati-Atihan Festival and Dinagyang Festival to the lively Manggahan Festival, Salakayan Festival,
and Tultugan Festival, each one reflects the heart and pride of the community.
